The basic idea is to provide a template

JiraReportTemplate:
'''
h4. Preliminary Analysis

{{block:begin:X}}{{data:evidence_description}}

{code}
{{block:begin:Y}}{{data:extracontent}}{{columnData:h1.ts}}:{{columnData:h2.msg}}
{{block:end:Y}}
{code}

{{block:end:X}}
'''

----

block:X
  * evidence_description: the text

Block:Y
  * datarow
  
last both together for a non prolonged comment

---
  
Block:Y
  * datarow


for a prolonged comment

---
  
Block:Y with 
  * datarow
  * extracontent: "[..]\n"

for an skip comment.
  
---
  
  
VideoReportTemplate:
'''
{{block:begin:X}}h5. Analysis of [^{{data:videofilename}}]

The length of the video is {{data:videolength}}:
{{block:begin:Y}}* {{data:evidence_description}}
{{block:end:Y}}
{{block:begin:X}}
'''

block:X
  * videofilename
  * videolength

block:Y
  * evidence_description
